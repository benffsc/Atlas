TIPPY DEMO QUESTIONS FOR PIP
============================

These questions are designed to showcase Tippy's ability to tell stories from 
our data - not just report numbers, but explain what they mean and highlight 
the human element behind our work.

Note: Tippy will add appropriate caveats about data limitations (e.g., "100% of 
cats we've seen" rather than claiming absolute completeness).


SUCCESS STORIES
---------------

1. What do we know about Pozzan Road in Healdsburg?
   (Emily West's 24-cat mass trapping - our showcase success story)

2. Tell me about 175 Scenic Avenue in Santa Rosa
   (Large established colony with high alteration rate)

3. What happened at the Silveira Ranch?
   (Farm/ranch TNR operation)

4. Show me our biggest single-day trapping events
   (Mass trapping highlights - demonstrates coordinated effort)

5. Who are our most active caretakers?
   (Human stories behind the numbers)


IMPACT & OVERVIEW
-----------------

6. How many cats has FFSC helped overall?
   (Big picture impact - 36,000+ cats, 88% alteration rate)

7. Which city in Sonoma County has the most cats in our system?
   (Geographic breakdown - Santa Rosa leads)

8. What's our alteration rate across all colonies?
   (Scientific context - above 70% threshold means population stabilization)

9. How does Santa Rosa compare to Petaluma?
   (City comparison showing consistent impact)

10. What do we know about the Roseland area - zip code 95407?
    (Underserved community, high density work)


PRIORITY & ACTION
-----------------

11. What locations need attention right now?
    (Active requests with untrapped cats remaining)

12. Are there places where caretakers reported more cats than we've trapped?
    (Gap between reported and verified - clear priorities)

13. Which colonies have the lowest alteration rates?
    (With caveat about data quality - NULL status vs confirmed intact)

14. Where should we focus trapping resources next month?
    (Strategic prioritization based on evidence)

15. Are there any active requests that have been waiting a long time?
    (Overdue requests needing attention)


GEOGRAPHIC CLUSTERS
-------------------

16. Tell me about TNR activity in West County
    (Sebastopol, Forestville, Guerneville area)

17. What's happening in the Russian River area?
    (Guerneville, Monte Rio - seasonal population challenges)

18. Are there any hot zones we should know about?
    (Clusters of activity like Pozzan Road area)

19. Which areas might have cats but little data?
    (Transparency about coverage gaps - rural, wealthy areas)


DATA TRANSPARENCY
-----------------

20. What don't we know about cats in Sonoma County?
    (Honest assessment of limitations - we only know what comes through 
    our clinic, actual population is likely higher)


TIPS FOR THE DEMO
-----------------

- Start with #1 (Pozzan Road) - it's the strongest story
- Follow with #6 (overall impact) for the big picture
- Use #11 or #12 to show operational value
- End with #20 to demonstrate honest, trustworthy AI

Tippy will:
- Tell stories, not just report numbers
- Add context about what the numbers mean
- Include honest caveats about data limitations
- Sound like a knowledgeable colleague, not a robot
